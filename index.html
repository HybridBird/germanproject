Crazy German Laws

  1. It is illegal to address a police officer with 'du', the informal form of you - you must use 'sie' instead.
  2. It is illegal to run out of fuel on the Autobahn.
  3. You can only mow the lawn on Monday to Saturday, from 7am to 8pm (due to the excessive noise levels)
  4. It is illegal to keep urns with ashes at home.
  5. There are many different penalties for insulting someone during road rage, ranging from 150€ for sticking out your tongue, to 2500€ for calling someone an old pig. (these are not fixed, but are what fines have been handed down by court judgements
